# Privacy Policy for Calendo
Last updated: June 2026
Calendo ("the app") is a calendar and task management app.
## Data we collect
- Google account email (only if you choose to connect Google Calendar)
- Calendar events and tasks you create — stored locally on your device
## Data we do not collect
- We do not collect personal data without your consent
- We do not sell your data to third parties
- We do not track you across other apps
## Google Calendar sync
If you connect your Google account, we access your Google Calendar data solely to display and sync events within the app. We do not store your Google data on any external server.
## Ads
The free version displays ads served by Google AdMob. Google's privacy policy applies to ad serving: https://policies.google.com/privacy
